Genral Services

Tradelync offers reliable and high-quality general services tailored for the hospitality and corporate sectors. Our solutions include hotel amenities, professional staff uniforms, cleaning and contracting services and premium laundry facilities — all designed to support daily operations with consistency, hygiene, and style. We are committed to delivering practical, cost-effective services that uphold your brand’s standards.

Hotel Amenities Supply

We offer a curated range of luxury and essential hotel amenities designed to enhance guest experiences. Our product range includes.

  • Toiletries (shampoo, body wash, lotion, etc.)
  • Dental and shaving kits
  • Bathrobes, slippers, and towels
  • In-room accessories (kettles, stationery, sewing kits)
  • Eco-friendly and sustainable product options

    • Professional Uniform Solutions

    We design and supply high-quality uniforms that reflect your brand image and meet operational needs.

  • Front desk and concierge uniforms
  • Housekeeping and maintenance uniforms
  • Chef and kitchen staff attire

  • Custom embroidery and logo printing available

    • Cleaning & Contracting Services
